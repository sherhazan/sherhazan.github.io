## Abstract

Our team at Bosch Corporate Research develops end-to-end solutions for robotic manipulation, leveraging state-of-the-art AI techniques to tackle industrial assembly challenges. AI4IA enables precise, scalable, and robust robotic assembly, ensuring adaptability to real-world industrial environments.
